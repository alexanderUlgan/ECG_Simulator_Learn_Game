ECG Signal Game

ECG Signal Game, developed on the Kahoot platform, is an exciting and educational experience that challenges players to improve their skills in identifying heart diseases through electrocardiography (ECG) readings. Players must select the correct heart disease associated with various heart patterns on the screen, from arrhythmias to heart blocks.
